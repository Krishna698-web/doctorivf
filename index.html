IVF Process Step by Step

1. Ovarian Stimulation

The woman receives fertility medications to stimulate multiple egg production.

2. Egg Retrieval

Eggs are collected from the ovaries using a minimally invasive procedure.

3. Sperm collection

The male partner provides a sperm sample or donor sperm is used.

4. Fertilization

Egs and sperm are combined in the laboratory to facilitate fertilization.

5. Embryo Culture

Fertilized eggs (embryos) are cultured for a few days to develop further.

6. Embryo Transfer

One or more embryos are carefully place into the woman's uterus.

7. Harmonal Suppport

Hormonal support is given to aid embryo implantation into womens body.

8. Pregnancy Test

A blood test is performed to determine if pregnancy has occurred.

+ What is In Vitro Fertilization(IVF)?

A process of fertilizing an egg with sperm from outside the woman's body. The egg is then placed back inside her uterus to develop.

+ Is In Vitro Fertilization Expensive?

Similar to other complex medical treatments, IVF involves highly skilled professionals, advanced laboratories, and equipment. Sometimes, multiple cycles may be necessary to achieve success. Therefore, it can be a significant investment.
