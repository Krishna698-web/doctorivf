Our Services

We provide personalized fertility consultations, cutting-edge reproductive technologies, and compassionate support throughout your journey to parenthood.

IVF-ICSI (Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection)

This is a highly specialized technique in infertility treatment, wherein a morphologically optimal sperm is injected into the egg and cultured in an incubator to form an embryo. This process requires a highly skilled embryologist and a tailored stimulation protocol, where the patient receives hormonal injections for 10 days followed by a trigger. Success rates vary across different centers, but with our expertise and robust Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), we consistently achieve a success rate of 80% or higher.

IUI â€” Intra-uterine Insemination

IUI is a straightforward infertility treatment. The process begins with ovarian stimulation using tablets and minimal injections, monitored through serial ultrasound scans. Once the follicle reaches a size of 18-20 mm, a trigger injection is administered, and IUI is scheduled after 36 hours. Semen preparation involves collecting ejaculate, processing it in the andrology laboratory using density gradient media. The processed and washed sample is then inseminated into the uterus through the vagina using a simple, non-anesthetic technique. A pregnancy test is conducted after 15 days, supported by luteal phase support.

PGS/PGD (Preimplantation Genetic Screening/ Diagnosis)

This technique proves to be a boon for couples facing multiple failed IVFs and recurrent implantation failure. The procedure entails biopsying the day 5 embryo (blastocyst), conducting various genetic tests to diagnose multiple inherited diseases, aneuploidies, and rare genetic conditions.

TESA/TESE/MICRO-TESE (Testicular Sperm Aspiration/ Extraction)

Is a surgical procedure used to retrieve sperm directly from the testicles, especially in cases of non-obstructive azoospermia where natural sperm production is insufficient. Typically performed under anesthesia, this involves removing a small piece of testicular tissue for examination and identification of viable sperm. This minimally invasive day care surgery is vital for individuals with conditions like testicular failure. The retrieved sperm can be utilized in assisted reproductive technologies such as in vitro fertilization (IVF) or intracytoplasmic sperm injection (ICSI) to aid in achieving pregnancy.

Laparoscopy

Laparoscopy is performed under general anesthesia, ensuring a pain-free experience for the patient. The surgeon makes small incisions in the abdomen to insert a laparoscope, miniature surgical tools, and a tube for abdominal gas infusion. This facilitates enhanced visibility and precision during the procedure. Following the completion of the laparoscopy, the abdominal gas is released, incisions are closed with stitches, and a dressing is applied. In many cases, patients can return home on the same day, although overnight hospital stay may be necessary in some instances.

Hysteroscopy

Hysteroscopy is a minimally invasive procedure for diagnosing and treating abnormal uterine bleeding. Using a hysteroscope inserted through the vagina, surgeons examine the cervix and uterus. This technique allows for the removal of polyps, fibroids, and adhesions during operative hysteroscopy, providing an effective and targeted approach to address uterine health concerns.
